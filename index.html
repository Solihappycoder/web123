Our Mission

Burns Creek High School is a school of choice within the Roblox schooling community. We offer a unique experience - a realistic and engaging school based in Perth, Australia.

We are constantly thinking of new ways to keep the school engaging and maintain our large player base which ranges from 45-80 students depending on the particular day.

Students have the opportunity to receive quality education in a modern indoor-outdoor campus. We are proud to offer multiple specialist facilities - some that are unseen in any other Roblox school within the circle. These include:

  • Hospitality Training Centre
  • Senior School Hub with a lecture hall, common room and careers office
  • Sports Hall
  • Science Laboratories
  • Performing Arts Theatre
  • Dance Studio
  • Student Services Support Hub

Burns Creek High School has developed a strong reputation within the community after we first reopened in 2022 - but our roots trace back to mid-2020. After providing education for so long, the school is able to label itself as a highly established school, with an executive team that knows what works and what doesn't. This has contributed to the success of the school in recent years.

Our Performance

Burns Creek High School produced satisfactory results in recent NAPLAN testing for Year 7 and Year 9 students.

Below are the average results for each NAPLAN test conducted:

  • Numeracy Y7: 3 out of 5
  • Numeracy Y9: 1 out of 5
  • Language Y7: 2.5 out of 5
  • Language Y9: 3 out of 5
  • Writing: 6 out of 10

These results allowed Head of Departments to understand where curriculums need adjustments based on where students are struggling.
